When analyzing competitors or writing competitive content, follow these rules.

## Main Competitors

1. **Jasper** — AI writing tool focused on content generation
2. **Copy.ai** — AI copywriting for marketing teams
3. **Writer** — AI writing platform for enterprises

## Analysis Framework

Cover these five areas for each competitor:

1. **Positioning** — How do they describe themselves? What's their main promise?
2. **Strengths** — What do they do better than us? Be honest.
3. **Weaknesses** — Where do they fall short?
4. **Pricing** — How does their pricing compare? What's the value perception?
5. **Audience** — Who are they targeting? Where's the overlap with our audience?

## Rules

- Never trash competitors — acknowledge their strengths, then differentiate
- Focus on what we do differently, not what they do wrong
- Use specific comparisons ("we do X, they do Y") not vague claims ("we're better")
- Always back claims with evidence: features, pricing, reviews, case studies
- Update competitive data quarterly — old data is worse than no data
