## Primary Buyer

Heads of marketing and VP-level marketers at B2B SaaS companies with 50–500 employees.

They care about:
- Proving marketing ROI to leadership
- Scaling content and campaigns without growing headcount
- Reducing time spent on repetitive reporting and optimization tasks
- Staying on top of multiple ad platforms and channels

Their pain points:
- Drowning in data but lacking actionable insights
- Too many tools, not enough integration
- Agency costs are high but output quality is inconsistent
- No time for strategic work because tactical work eats the day

## Secondary Audience

Marketing managers and senior marketers who do the hands-on execution. They're often the ones who discover the tool and champion it internally.

## Exclusions

- Enterprise companies (500+ employees) — different buying process, need custom solutions
- SMBs under 20 employees — usually no dedicated marketing team
- Agencies — different use case and pricing model
