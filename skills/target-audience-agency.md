## Primary Buyer

Agency owners and directors at digital marketing agencies with 5-50 employees managing 10-100 client accounts.

They care about:
- Reducing time spent on repetitive client reporting
- Scaling client load without proportional headcount growth
- Maintaining quality and consistency across all client accounts
- Differentiating from other agencies with better tooling

Their pain points:
- Junior staff spend 40%+ of their time pulling and formatting reports
- Every client wants a custom report format but there's no time to build them
- Managing 10+ ad platform logins across dozens of clients
- Client onboarding takes weeks to get all integrations connected

## Secondary Audience

Account managers and media buyers who do the daily execution. They feel the pain most acutely and push for better tools internally.

## Messaging Rules

- Lead with time savings and margin improvement
- Frame features as "for your clients" not just "for you"
- Reference multi-client workflows: bulk reporting, cross-account views, client-scoped access
- Agencies are skeptical of tools that promise to replace them - position as augmenting their team

## Exclusions

- Solo freelancers managing fewer than 3 clients
- PR-only agencies (different workflow entirely)
- Agencies focused on organic-only with no paid media management
